# Andrej Karpathy

**TL;DR:** Slovak-Canadian AI researcher and educator, co-founder of OpenAI and former Director of AI at Tesla. Widely known for accessible deep learning education (Neural Networks: Zero to Hero) and influential open-source work on LLMs and neural net training.

---

## Role

Independent researcher and educator (as of 2024–2026). Previously:
- **OpenAI** — co-founder and research scientist (2015–2017; returned 2022–2023)
- **Tesla** — Senior Director of AI / Autopilot Vision (2017–2022)

## Key contributions

| Work | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Neural Networks: Zero to Hero** | YouTube lecture series building neural nets from scratch; widely used in self-study |
| **nanoGPT** | Minimal, readable GPT-2 implementation in ~300 lines; standard reference for learning transformers |
| **micrograd** | Tiny autograd engine; teaches backpropagation from first principles |
| **Karpathy's LLM Wiki idea file** | Pattern for building persistent personal knowledge bases using LLMs (see [[llm-wiki-pattern]]) |
| **"The Unreasonable Effectiveness of RNNs"** (2015) | Influential blog post demonstrating character-level RNN capabilities |
| **Tesla Autopilot** | Led transition to vision-only (camera-only) autonomous driving stack |

## Intellectual style

Known for building things from scratch to understand them deeply ("from-scratch" pedagogy), strong preference for readable minimal code over production frameworks, and public communication of ideas via GitHub gists and informal blog posts rather than formal papers.
